About Me

I am a B.Com graduate who transitioned into the creative field by completing a UI/UX and Graphic Design course. Over the past 2 years and 11 months, I have worked as a UI/UX and Graphic Designer, gaining strong experience in designing websites, mobile applications, and digital branding assets. My core expertise lies in user interface design, wireframing, prototyping, and responsive layouts, with proficiency in tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I have collaborated with cross-functional teams including developers and marketing professionals, ensuring that every design is both visually appealing and user-friendly. While working in my company, I also developed an interest in web development and started learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and WordPress. This hands-on experience helped me contribute to frontend tasks, theme customization, and website improvements, bridging the gap between design and development. With this blend of creative design expertise and growing technical knowledge, I bring a well-rounded approach to projects—focusing on both user experience and practical implementation. I am passionate about continuous learning and eager to take on new challenges that allow me to build meaningful and impactful digital solutions.
